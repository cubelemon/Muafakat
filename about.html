Background

The Muafakat Games 2024, an annual sports event hosted by Malaysian students in Australia, gains direct support from Education Malaysia Australia and the High Commissioner. It unites students and alumni nationwide, transcending differences in religion, race, and background for spirited sporting competitions. This year, the Event debuted as a rotating host location among regions; NSW, VIC, QLD, and ACT, starting in Sydney, NSW. A student council formed by leaders from these regions spearheads this inaugural event, setting the stage for a lasting annual tradition.
